What We're About

Contraptions That Actually Work

CreatingSMP is built on the Create mod — a mechanical engineering sandbox that turns Minecraft into a world of rotating shafts, conveyor belts, windmill-powered factories, and elaborate automated farms. Every machine you see was built by a real player. Nothing is spawned in.

Create Mod Core

The Founder

The Person Behind the Gears

Tako is the founder and developer behind CreatingSMP — a California-based creator, modder, and developer who's been building things in Minecraft long before contraptions were cool. The server isn't just a hobby project; it's a carefully crafted world built on years of technical experience (and an unhealthy number of hours spent staring at gear ratios).

When he isn't managing the server, you'll find him building mods, publishing Minecraft info content on TakoStreamYT, developing flight simulator aircraft add-ons, or doing something involving gear ratios and way too many cogwheels.

What Drives CreatingSMP

The idea behind the server is simple: give players the tools to be genuinely creative, then get out of the way. No linear quest lines, no hand-holding, no prescribed goal. Just a massive world, a powerful modpack, and the freedom to build whatever you can imagine.

Tako obsesses over the details — the choice of add-ons, the biome generation, the balance between exploration and automation. Everything on the server is intentional. Except for the lag spikes. Those are just contraptions being contraptions.
